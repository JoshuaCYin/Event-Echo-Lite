About EventEcho

Your comprehensive platform for discovering, organizing, and managing campus events

Our Mission

EventEcho was created to simplify event management for campus communities. We believe that staying connected and engaged shouldn't be complicated. Our platform brings together event organizers and attendees in one seamless experience.

What We Offer

  • Centralized event calendar for all campus activities
  • Easy RSVP and attendance tracking
  • AI-powered event recommendations tailored to your interests
  • Powerful planning tools for event organizers
  • Real-time notifications and updates
  • Mobile-friendly design for on-the-go access

For Students

Never miss an event that matters to you. Browse upcoming activities, RSVP with one click, and receive personalized recommendations based on your interests. Stay connected with your campus community and make the most of your college experience.

For Organizers

Streamline your event planning process with our intuitive tools. Create and manage events, track attendance, send notifications, and analyze engagement—all from one dashboard. Our AI assistant can even help you plan better events based on past data.
